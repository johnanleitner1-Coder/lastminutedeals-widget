Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

What We Collect

When you use our booking chat widget, we collect only the information needed to process your tour booking:

  • Name, email, phone number — required to create your booking and send confirmation
  • Chat messages — your conversation with the booking assistant, used to understand your request and provide relevant tour options
  • Payment information — processed securely by Stripe; we never see or store your card details

How We Use It

  • Process and confirm your tour booking
  • Send booking confirmation and relevant updates
  • Provide customer support if issues arise
  • Improve the booking experience (aggregated, anonymized analytics)

Who We Share It With

  • Tour operator — your name, email, phone, and booking details are shared with the tour operator to fulfill your booking
  • Stripe — payment processing only
  • Anthropic — your chat messages are processed by Claude AI to generate responses; Anthropic does not retain or train on this data

We do not sell your data or share it with advertisers.

Data Retention

  • Chat conversations are automatically deleted after 90 days
  • Booking records are retained for 6 years as required by tax law

Your Rights

You have the right to request deletion of your data at any time. Contact us and we will delete your conversation history and personal information (except booking records required by law).

Cookies & Tracking

We do not use cookies. A session token is stored in your browser's local storage to maintain your conversation. No third-party tracking, no fingerprinting, no analytics scripts.

Contact

For privacy questions or data deletion requests: privacy@lastminutedealshq.com
