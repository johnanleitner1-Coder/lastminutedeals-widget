Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Who We Are

This booking widget is operated by Last Minute Deals HQ LLC on behalf of the tour operator whose website you are visiting. The tour operator is the data controller (they determine why your data is processed). We are the data processor (we handle the data on their behalf to provide the booking service).

What We Collect

When you use this booking assistant, we collect only the information needed to process your tour booking:

  • Name, email, phone number — required to create your booking and send confirmation
  • Chat messages — your conversation with the AI booking assistant, used to understand your request and provide relevant tour options
  • Payment information — processed securely by Stripe; we never see or store your card details

AI Disclosure

This booking assistant is powered by artificial intelligence. You are communicating with an AI system, not a human. Your messages are processed by OpenAI and Anthropic to generate responses. These providers process your messages under their respective data processing agreements and do not use your conversations to train their models.

Lawful Basis for Processing

We process your personal data under the following legal bases (GDPR Article 6):

  • Performance of a contract — your name, email, phone, and booking details are processed to fulfill the tour booking you request
  • Legitimate interest — chat messages are retained for dispute resolution and service improvement; aggregated, anonymized usage data is used to improve the booking experience

How We Use It

  • Process and confirm your tour booking
  • Share booking details with the tour operator for fulfillment
  • Provide customer support if issues arise
  • Improve the booking experience (aggregated, anonymized analytics)

Who We Share It With

  • Tour operator — your name, email, phone, and booking details are shared with the tour operator to fulfill your booking
  • Stripe — payment processing only (Stripe is PCI DSS compliant)
  • OpenAI and Anthropic — your chat messages are processed by AI systems to generate responses
  • Railway — infrastructure hosting (servers located in the United States)

We do not sell your data or share it with advertisers.

International Data Transfers

Your data is processed on servers in the United States. Where data is transferred outside the European Economic Area, we rely on Standard Contractual Clauses and the data processing agreements of our service providers to ensure adequate protection.

Data Retention

  • Conversations that result in a booking are retained for the duration of the booking record (up to 6 years as required by tax and commercial law)
  • Conversations that do not result in a booking are deleted after 90 days
  • Anonymized analytics data (no personal information) is retained indefinitely

Your Rights

Under GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of your personal data
  • Rectification — correct inaccurate data
  • Erasure — request deletion of your data (except records required by law)
  • Restriction — limit how we process your data
  • Portability — receive your data in a structured format
  • Objection — object to processing based on legitimate interest

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the address below.

Cookies & Tracking

We do not use cookies. A session token is stored in your browser's local storage to maintain your conversation. No third-party tracking, no fingerprinting, no analytics scripts.
